How to use the Excel template?

The macro can be used to upload any tab separated data from Excel to the Sia Skynet Cloud and get an Skylink in return. With the same link you can later also download the data from the Sia Skynet Cloud directly into your Excel sheet.

Select Data

Select data in your Excel sheet which should be uploaded to Sia Skynet.

Upload Data

After selecting the data in the sheet click the Upload button to upload the data to Sia Skynet.

Get Skylink

When the upload is complete you will get back a Skylink that you can share with others.

Download Data

You can also use exisitng Skylinks from previous uploads to retrieve data. Simply copy the Skylink into the field and click the Download button.
